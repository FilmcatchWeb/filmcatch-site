How it works

Walk your city. Catch real movies. Build your collection.

Everything in FilmCatch happens in the real world. Here's the full loop — scan, walk, catch, collect, trade.

01

Scan your area

Open the map and scan to reveal films hidden near your real location. Spawns fill the blocks around you — pan the map and scan new areas to reveal even more.

Good to know
Spawns rotate through the day, so the same corner can surprise you tomorrow.
02

Walk into range

Films are catchable once you’re within 200 metres. The reach ring around you shows exactly what’s in range — everything else is a reason to keep walking.

Good to know
Stay aware of your surroundings — never catch and drive.
03

Make the catch

Tap a spawn to see the film, then catch it. Every catch is a reveal moment — rarity, genre badges, and full film details, straight onto your shelf.

Good to know
Any catch can roll a holographic Shiny with animated foil.
04

Build your collection

Browse your catches in a grid, filter by rarity, and complete themed Sets. Duplicates aren’t clutter — fuse three copies into a higher tier, or flip them on the market.

Good to know
Completing a Set earns rewards and permanent bragging rights.
05

Trade with collectors

List any film and set your Ticket price, or run a live auction with a Buy Now option. Gift films, Tickets, and Gems to friends to grow your crew.

Good to know
Trades are protected — cheaters get reversed and suspended.

How rarity works

A film’s tier is earned from its real-world prestige — critical acclaim and popularity decide how rare it spawns. And after dark, the horror films come out to play.

And then there's the Shiny.
Any catch can roll holographic — an animated foil variant. Rare enough that you'll remember where you were.
And the PREMIERE window.
Brand-new releases spawn as gold-badged PREMIERE cards for their first 90 days only. Catch one in the window and the badge is yours forever.
